Policy & Society

Keep civic updates, public policy briefs, and social impact reports synchronized across regions. intxtonic automates localization while giving policy teams the context controls and approval gates they need.

Regional publishing flows

Route policy updates through per-region workflows with custom approvers, legal checks, and publishing channels. Ensure every locale receives timely announcements that match local terminology.

  • Configurable review chains per country or jurisdiction.
  • Locale-specific metadata requirements (legal citations, agencies, deadlines).
  • Automatic handoff to downstream public sites or newsletters.

Stakeholder portals

Provide regulators, NGOs, and community partners with tailored dashboards summarizing the policies that affect them. Combine translations with commentary, timelines, and open actions.

  • Role-based content filters and permission sets.
  • Action item trackers with due dates and follow-up flows.
  • Embedded discussion threads for feedback loops.

Contextual analytics

Monitor the reach and reception of policy content. Track which locales engage, which pages need updates, and how sentiment shifts across languages.

  • Engagement dashboards segmented by locale and stakeholder type.
  • Auto-detected sentiment summaries with drill-down to source comments.
  • Alerts for outdated or inconsistent policy references.
